What is Guided Learning?

Guided Learning is an elective subject where students can take control of their own learning in an area they're interested in. Students will explore topics that build on what they already know or help them learn something new.

They'll plan and carry out their own learning project with guidance, working step by step beyond the usual classroom setting. In the end, they'll create a portfolio that shows what they've learned and how they've grown. All of this would be carried out under the Plan-Perform-Monitor-Reflect process.
